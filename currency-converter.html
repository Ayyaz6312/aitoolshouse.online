Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about currency conversion

How does the currency converter work?
Our currency converter fetches real-time exchange rate data from reliable financial sources and instantly calculates conversions between 206 world currencies. Simply select your source and target currencies, enter the amount, and get accurate results updated every hour.
How many currencies are supported?
ToolsHouse Currency Converter supports 206 world currencies, including all major currencies like USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR, CNY, as well as currencies from every country and territory worldwide, covering all ISO 4217 active currency codes.
Is this currency converter free to use?
Yes, the ToolsHouse Currency Converter is completely free to use with no registration, no sign-up, and no hidden fees. You get unlimited currency conversions with real-time exchange rates at zero cost.
How often are exchange rates updated?
Exchange rates are updated approximately every 60 minutes from reliable financial data providers. This ensures you always get the most accurate and current conversion rates for all 206 supported currencies.
Can I use this for business or commercial purposes?
While our converter provides accurate real-time rates, we recommend using it for informational and estimation purposes. For actual financial transactions, always verify rates with your bank or financial institution as they may apply different rates and fees.
What is an exchange rate?
An exchange rate is the value of one currency expressed in terms of another currency. For example, if the USD to EUR exchange rate is 0.92, it means 1 US Dollar can be exchanged for 0.92 Euros. Exchange rates fluctuate based on market conditions.
